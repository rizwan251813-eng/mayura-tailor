Mayura Tailor is a trusted name in premium tailoring, proudly serving the community of Hirgana, Mururu, Udupi. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques to deliver garments that fit perfectly and last beautifully.

Whether you need an elegant blouse, a sharp formal shirt, or a custom ethnic outfit — we bring precision and passion to every stitch. Our expert tailors ensure your clothes feel as good as they look.
